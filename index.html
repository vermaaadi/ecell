About Us

We at E-Cell,NIT Silchar promotes Entrepreneurship, Search Business Minded Peoples and Structure them to be Job Providers instead of Job Seekers. E-Cell NIT Silchar has produced well known Entrepreneur, which are succeeding heavily in their respective fields. We Help with Ideas, with Teams, with Platforms and with Funds. We conduct wide variety of events focussed on Training Entrepreneurs and keeping them upto date about Market to Structure them to Compete and Excel in Competitive Market. Join us and earn the Lessons which will take you a long way.

Our Events

What we Do

E-Cell organises a variety of fun and entrepreneurial events throughout the year in and around the campus.

Srijan Events

Guest Lecture

From Scratch to Top, what one needs most is right Guidance. But Who are the one's who will provide you with this Guidance? The one who has passed through this phase, right! Aiming this SRIJAN 1.0 presents Guest Lectures to guide budding Entrepreneurs to Achieve Success, to Train them to deal with Situation and accepting and getting over Challenges.

Pitching Competition

SRIJAN 1.0 presents Pitching competition, for all the entrepreneurial enthusiasts looking for mentor-ship, funding and other opportunities that could give them the big break. Come up with your ideas and pitch it in front of a panel of experts and judges and let them examine your ability to evaluate the market, customer needs, product usability.

Board Room

Imagined yourself as Board Member? Srijan provides an amazing opportunity for you to actually get acquainted with what a CEO,CTO and COO does. Here you get to learn to face challenges that you might have to, one day when you sit on the board of directors of your own company! The thought itself is amazing, isn't it?

Business Model Canvas Competition

Srijan 1.0 brings to you 'Business Model Canvas Competition' which will facilitate the student entrepreneurs to learn about the strategic management of business and showcase their potentials in this arena.The Business Model Canvas gives people a common language through which they can evaluate traditional processes and bring innovation into their business models. It is how you ‘paint’ your business ideas as creatively as possible.

Stock Market Workshop

We are very much familiar with the term, but the details of Stock Market are a mystery. SRIJAN 1.0 brings you Stock Market Workshop where one will be learning the basics and would be able start exploring this seemingly much attractive domain. Not only the knowledge, but you will also get a real-life trading experience.

Business Quiz

Do you know the names of all the Unicorn companies that are start-ups? Or is business trivia always on your browser history? If yes, then SRIJAN 1.0 "Business Quiz" is just the place for you to prove your mettle, while competing with the finest business-enthusiasts from all over the country.

IPL Auction

Ever imagined what it would be like to be a part of a live auction? Then you’ve come to the right place. Welcome to the IPL Auction, an event SRIJAN 1.0, where you can put yourself in the shoes of a bidder to build your dream team from scratch to lift the IPL trophy with.

Comedy Night

After so much effort that you’ve put into various competitions this week, it’s time to put your mind to ease. Humor is the perfect combination of wits and creativity, it is a science that requires keen observation and a phenomenal grasp of human behaviour. Helping you relax your minds after days of hard work, SRIJAN 1.0 brings to you KISHORE DAYANI, as part of its “Comedy Night”.
